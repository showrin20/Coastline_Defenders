Overview

We aim to streamline disaster management through effective resource allocation, user-friendly dashboards, and comprehensive reporting tools that bolster decision-making efficiency.

Comprehensive Monitoring of River Erosion

Stay ahead of environmental challenges with tools to monitor erosion along the Jamuna River. Use satellite imagery, predictive models, and data visualization to track changes, empowering communities with insights to mitigate erosion impacts.

Tailored Erosion Mitigation Plans

Enroll in our Erosion Mitigation Program (EMP) to receive customized action plans. Use AI-powered analysis to assess riverbank risks and access real-time recommendations to respond effectively.

Geospatial Tools for Flood Risk Management

Visualize riverbank changes with real-time geospatial tools. Identify high-risk zones and plan evacuations with offline mapping features for better disaster management.

Real-Time River Monitoring and Predictive Analysis

Leverage AI and satellite data for real-time monitoring. Predict future erosion trends and generate actionable insights to protect vulnerable communities.
