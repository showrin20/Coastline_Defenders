Discover Our Mission

Our mission is to combat the relentless riverbank erosion impacting communities along the Jamuna River basin. We focus on empowering these communities through effective resource allocation, early warning systems, and intuitive dashboards to help safeguard agricultural land, infrastructure, and livelihoods from the destructive forces of the river.

Overview

Addressing the severe consequences of Jamuna River erosion, we strive to improve resilience in vulnerable areas. Our approach includes deploying predictive tools for river flow, creating standardized data frameworks, and delivering comprehensive reporting systems.
